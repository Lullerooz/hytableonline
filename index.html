Progression System

üìà Experience Gain

XP is calculated based on mob health. Stronger mobs give more experience, encouraging you to take on greater challenges as you level up.

üéØ Level Requirements

Each level requires more XP than the last, using a quadratic progression system. Early levels are quick, while higher levels require dedication and grinding.

üíé Stat Points

Earn 5 stat points every time you level up. At max level (100), you'll have 495 stat points to allocate across all 8 stats, allowing for deep character customization.

Server Commands

Essential commands to get you started

Essentials

/spawn Teleport to spawn
/home Teleport to your home
/sethome <name> Set a home location
/delhome <name> Delete a home
/warp <name> Teleport to a warp
/tpa <player> Request teleport to player
/tpahere <player> Request player to teleport to you
/repair Repair your items (12hr cooldown)

Economy

/balance Check your Merits balance
/pay <player> <amount> Pay another player
/shop Open the shop menu
/auction Access auction house
/job View available jobs

Kits & Items

/kit <name> Claim a kit (rank-based)
/crates Open crate menu
/rtp Random teleport

Social

/msg <player> <message> Send private message
/shout <message> Broadcast message to all players
/afk Toggle AFK status
